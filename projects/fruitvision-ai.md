# FruitVision AI - Fruit Quality Detector for SMS

Repository: https://github.com/mohammednafees1007-hub/fruit-quality-detector-for-sms

## Summary

FruitVision AI is a quality-first fruit grading system for SMS. It analyzes upload or camera images and returns quality, grade, fruit-name context, and annotated visual output.

## What Was Built

- FastAPI backend.
- YOLOv8x fruit/object detection support.
- Keras quality classifier path.
- CLIP fruit-name support.
- Next.js frontend for detection workflow.
- Fresh/Adulterant/Rotten quality output mapped to A/B/C grades.

## Resume Bullet

Built a quality-first fruit grading website that analyzes upload or camera images, predicts Fresh/Adulterant/Rotten quality, maps A/B/C grades, and displays annotated results.
